♫ What I charge: ♫

My lessons are 30 minutes, once a week, usually daily between 2 and 5:30 pm. I am flexible to the student/parent needs within reason. If a student needs hour lessons, or times not offered this can be discussed. I charge $22.50 per half hour, which works out to be $90, paid in full at the beginning of each month.
