♫How I teach: ♫

I use the Bastien and Alfred Piano Method books. I feel it is important to learn all you can about music instead of just how to play, which is why I teach theory, ear training, sight reading, scales, arpeggios, cadence and more. Once a year we have a recital, usually in the Spring. I have incentive programs to make playing the piano fun, with games and prizes. Depending on how many students I have, I can offer group lessons, summer camps and Christmas parties. The ages I have taught range from 3 years old up to adults (all ages).
